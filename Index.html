Frequently asked questions :-
What is Sell on Amazon?

Sell on Amazon is a program that allows individuals and businesses to list and sell their products on the Amazon marketplace. Sellers can manage their sales through the Seller Central platform after registration.

How do I list my products on Amazon?

To list products, sellers need to complete their registration and then use the Seller Central dashboard to add their products. They can choose between different fulfillment methods, including Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) or self-ship.

Who handles shipping?

Shipping responsibilities depend on the fulfillment method chosen by the seller. With FBA, Amazon manages shipping and returns. If sellers opt for self-ship or Easy Ship, they are responsible for packaging and delivering the products.

How do I ask a question about a product?

Customers can ask questions by navigating to the product page and using the "Customer Questions & Answers" feature. They simply enter their question in the provided text box.

How do you handle an irritated customer?

To manage an irritated customer, it is crucial to listen actively to their concerns without interruption. Acknowledge their feelings and apologize for any inconvenience. Then, work collaboratively to find a solution that meets their needs.

What is the role of a customer service associate?

A customer service associate is responsible for addressing customer inquiries, resolving issues, processing orders, and providing product information. They act as the primary point of contact between the company and its customers.
